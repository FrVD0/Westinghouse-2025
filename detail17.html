Horizon and Gaze

Snowball Fight

By Queen Hodge

We were happy it snowed that night
And decided to have a snowball fight.
While lobbing the snowy orbs at each other
There was nowhere to run for cover
We threw them all over the place
Some of what they threw missed my face
Looking back as I recall
Mine always hit the brick wall
Unlike me, it was sturdy, and strong
It took a beating all day long
