Horizon and Gaze

Harvest the Day

By Margaret Devine-Sullivan

Why the rush?
Where are you going?
Sit. Rest. Sip.
Before memories slip.
Share them.
Stories of youth never get old.
Bitter and sweet, they nourish the soul.
Why the rush?
The sunset's glowing.
Sit. Rest. Sip.
Let's harvest the day.
