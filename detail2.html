Dancers

By Queen Hodge

Passionate Graceful Dancers
Every movement with boundless energy
Leaping swirling goddesses
Footloose, and fancy free
Play Any melody
Sultry Slow or rhythmic beats
Although some folks have tried
No one can compete
Passion for music is in their soles
They respond to the beat
Whether fast paced or
Painfully slow
They’re gonna move their butterfly feet
Taking every opportunity to dance
Their bodies soft yet strong
They’ll plié, bend, stretch and rise
And dance the whole day long
A burst of color with every step
Feathers of every hue
Nimble bodies shake a leg
A barefooted rendezvous
