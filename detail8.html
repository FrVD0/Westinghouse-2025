AT DAWN

By Queen Hodge

The rising sun beckons me to a path along the sea
Briny water ripples onto the rocky shore
then fades back
My breath aligns with the tide
As morning rays blossom through the misty clouds
I reach my arms toward the source
Renewed with an open heart
Nourished by the warmth of the glistening sky
Grateful for daylight and for new beginnings
The morning revives
The tide rises and fades
As the welcoming sunbeams break the spell of darkness
I am one with the breath, the tide and the rising sun
