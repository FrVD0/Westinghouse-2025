Death Of A Ketchup Packet

By Queen Hodge

Do you want to know why my french fries are wearing a frown?
Because its ketchup packet fell to the ground
Someone stepped on it and it splattered
My french fries pretended that it didn’t matter
Did someone pick up the damaged packet?
Was it thrown in the litter basket?
Sadly the packet died alone
Its final resting place unknown
And so my french fries sits lonely on a plate
Ketchup-less, that’s their fate
