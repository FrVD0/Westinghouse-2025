Horizon and Gaze

Oi 31

By Margaret Bleichman

for Manu Gonçalves

Hello Area 31, Minas Gerais.
Is anyone there? The payphone rings and rings
within its aging booth’s blue bonnet.
No one walks your hot midday concrete
or steps up onto the high sooty curb
built to rise above sudden tropical torrents.
A blaze of bright ochre and burnt umber walls
conceals cool courtyards and café com leite lives.

A cachorro merely two stairs tall
sits outside those walls, meditates
in a triangle of respite and focus,
body still, but ears at alert.
Like your early citizens, Minas, their
rebellions crushed again and again
until finally, liberation, we, too, will resist.
We persist, like the puppy who waits.

Two-year-old Manu, American girl
plucked from her birth land, to Minas Gerais
a ‘homeland’ she has never called home.
And how many others?
We will answer the call. We will step up.
We will rise like floodwaters to attain your motto:
Libertas quæ sera tamen:
Liberty, though it be delayed.

Notes:
Manu Gonçalves, an American citizen born in Ft. Lauderdale, Florida, was unconstitutionally deported to Minas Gerais, Brazil on February 21, 2025.
Minas Gerais: General Mines; a state in Brazil, with telephone area code 31
Oi: ‘Hi’
café com leite: coffee with milk; wealth and power derived from coffee and dairy industries (Brazilian political history)
cachorro: puppy
Libertas quæ sera tamen: motto on the Minas Gerais flag (Latin)
