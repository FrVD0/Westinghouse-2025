Horizon and Gaze

Abandoned building

By Queen Hodge

The bare bones of our whitewashed one room schoolhouse
The only structure to survive the violently rotating column of air that devastated our lives
The concrete shell
Its door unhinged
Empty of the chalkboard desks and chairs
Remembering not to forget the time when
Life was uncomplicated The memory of learned Lessons curiosity and vibrant dreams
Contaminated now by this stark reality
under the timeless and hopeful blue sky
